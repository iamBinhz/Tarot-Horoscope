The Tradition

Before You Draw

The tarot is not fortune-telling — it is a mirror of obsidian, a dark surface that reveals what the conscious mind has buried. Each card carries an archetypal resonance that speaks to the patterns woven through your life.

Approach with stillness and intention. The quality of your question shapes the clarity of the answer. Do not ask what will happen — ask what you need to understand. The cards respond to sincerity, not curiosity.

Three cards. Three windows into time: what was, what is, and what approaches. Together they form not a prediction, but a map of the soul's terrain.
