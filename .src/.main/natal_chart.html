What is Tử Vi Đẩu Số?

Originating in the Song Dynasty and attributed to the sage Trần Đoàn (Hi Di), Purple Star Astrology is the supreme art of Mệnh Học — mapping destiny through the movements of celestial bodies. It ranks alongside Bát Tự (Four Pillars) as the most revered system of fate-reading in Eastern metaphysics.

⚝

The 14 Main Stars

Fourteen Chính Tinh govern the chart: the imperial Tử Vi, strategic Thiên Cơ, radiant Thái Dương, resolute Vũ Khúc, gentle Thiên Đồng, ambitious Liêm Trinh, bountiful Thiên Phủ, luminous Thái Âm, charismatic Tham Lang, eloquent Cự Môn, noble Thiên Tướng, wise Thiên Lương, fierce Thất Sát, and revolutionary Phá Quân.

🏛

The 12 Palaces

Each palace represents a domain of life — from Mệnh (the self) and Tài Bạch (wealth), to Phu Thê (marriage) and Quan Lộc (career). Stars in each palace interact through Ngũ Hành relationships, creating unique Cách Cục patterns that define your potential.

🔄

Tứ Hóa — Four Transformations

The Tứ Hóa are energy modifiers based on your birth year's Heavenly Stem: Hóa Lộc brings abundance, Hóa Quyền grants authority, Hóa Khoa bestows wisdom, while Hóa Kỵ introduces friction — the universe's balancing force.

☯

Roots in Kinh Dịch

Tử Vi shares its philosophical DNA with the I Ching. Both rest upon Âm Dương and Ngũ Hành — the eternal dance of opposites and five elemental forces. The I Ching's principle of constant change lives in every Đại Hạn cycle.
